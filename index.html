The problem “the associated script cannot be loaded” occurs serval times when I use Unity. Therefore I search the Internet to find solutions, some solutions work, some not. Therefore, I am here making a summary.

1. Look at the Console and figure out what is wrong. If the Console is empty, try running the game. It will produce the error and you can then see it in the console.

2. Save project, close Unity, open it again.

3. Delete the Library and Temp folders in your project folder and re-open Unity. You won't lose anything important, but it will force Unity to re-import everything clean.

4. Rename the script.

5. Reimport all the scripts.

Players need to kill insects that fall from the top of screen to protect trees. In order to keep the tree growing up, the player should avoid killing the water droplets, and make sure the water can drop into the soil successfully. If the player kills the water droplets with the terminal, the water droplets will disappear and the terminal will temporarily lose its effect and can't generate laser. As the tree grows slowly, the distance between the insects reaching the tree is getting shorter and shorter, which means that the player has less space to kill the insects. Therefore, the game will become more and more difficult.
